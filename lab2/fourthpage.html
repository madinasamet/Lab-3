Benefits from reading books

It helps to expand your knowledge and understanding of the world,
improves cognitive function, reduces stress levels, increases empathy
and creativity, and provides an escape from everyday life.

It can also help increase empathy since it allows you to put yourself
in the shoes of different characters, better understanding their
motivations and feelings.
